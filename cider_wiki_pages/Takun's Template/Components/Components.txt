The [url]res://components[/url] contains some components that I think are universally good.

	[[>Health Component]] - For anything that needs health. Players, enemies, destructible objects, etc. 
	
	[[>Hitbox Component]] - For anything that needs to deal damage when an Area2D is entered.
	
	[[>Hurtbox Component]] - For anything that needs to take damage when an Area2D is entered.
	
	[[>Random Stream Component]] - For anything that needs to play Audio when an action takes place.
