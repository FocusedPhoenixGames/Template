The Sound Button plays a random sound when pressed.

[b][u]How to use[/u][/b]
	Simply add the different UI sounds to the RandomStreamComponent's Streams array and you're done.

[[img:img_1.webp]]

	By default the pitch will be randomozied between 0.9 and 1.1 to help add variety to the sounds. This can be turned off in the Inspector.
